## Task description

This task is a subset of ContractNLI, and consists of determinining whether a clause from an NDA has a particular legal effect.

## Task construction

## Classification question (verbatim from base_prompt.txt)

Identify if the clause provides that the Receiving Party shall notify
Disclosing Party in case Receiving Party is required by law, regulation
or judicial process to disclose any Confidential Information.
